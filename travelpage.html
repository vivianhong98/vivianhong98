Welcome to My Travel Diary!
June 2016 - Seattle, Washington

As part of the Hamilton Scholars Program, a group of students and I traveled to Seattle. We attended a series of leadership development and cultural humility workshops. We also explored the heart of Seattle, including Pike Place Market, the first Starbucks, and the Space Needle.
December 2017 - Guatemala City, Guatemala

As part of the Hamilton Scholars Program, a group of students and I traveled to Tecpan, Chimaltenango, Guatemala for one week. We taught English at a nearby Elementary public school and painted murals, revamping their school walls. We traveled to different rural areas of Guatemala, exploring their cultural traditions. We attended a Mayan ceremony, shadowed traditional agricultural and cloth-making techniques, and family-owned pottery and Guatemala chocolate industries.
December 2018 - Boston

My First Time in the Easy Coast. Last winter, my friend and I decided to spontaneously book a trip to Boston and New York. We lived with a friend in Northeastern University and explored Boston. We went to the New England Aquarium and ate dinner at a the Top of the Hub, which featured the beautiful landscapes of Boston.
